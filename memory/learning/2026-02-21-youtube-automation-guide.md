## 高效 Script Format

1. **Hook** (前5秒) - 引起好奇
   - 例: "Most people ignore this simple detail—and it's costing them more than they realize."
2. **Short setup** - 简述视频内容
3. **Main points** - 清晰、短句
4. **Ending** - 引起好奇心
